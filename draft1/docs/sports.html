Sports

Football is a very popular sport in this state. Pennsylvania is the home of 7 Superbowl titles. The Penn state nittany lions are often considered to be one of the better teams in college football.

Hockey has similar levels of popularity and like football, the state has claimed 7 stanley cup titles. Baseball is played throughout the state and coincidentally, the state also has 7 world series titles.
